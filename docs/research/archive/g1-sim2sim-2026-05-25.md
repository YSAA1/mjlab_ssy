  because the host session did not expose a readable `/dev/input/js0` joystick
  device.
- The non-clean automation-deviation lane produced useful sim2sim evidence and
  must remain labeled as `official_source_plus_automation_deviation`.
- Mode-15 deploy-scene mismatch was real: the official scene differed from the
  training/new-G1 model and the raw mode-15 lane missed official passive joint
  defaults.
- Applying actuator-limit alignment and official passive joint defaults removed
  the early mode-15 control blow-up.
- Standard-tracking continuation produced stable labeled sim2sim evidence for
  both flying-kick and roundhouse in the corrected mode-15 automation-deviation
  lane.
- The dual-kick real deploy wrapper was prepared but should not be treated as a
  hardware safety certification.
